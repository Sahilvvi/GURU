"Warriors of life"

The School Moto

Education is often considered as the supreme wealth among all kinds of wealth. Unlike material wealth, education cannot be taken away from an individual. It is a lifelong asset that provides a foundation for personal and professional growth. Education enables individuals to expand their knowledge, develop new skills, and gain a deeper understanding of the world around them.

Vision

Our vision is to create a community of learners where every student feels valued, supported, and inspired to achieve their full potential.

Mission

Our mission is to cultivate a community of lifelong learners who are curious, compassionate, and courageous in their pursuit of knowledge and personal growth. We strive to foster a culture of inclusivity and diversity, where every individual is valued and encouraged to reach their full potential.

Facilities

Computer lab

Highly equipped with modern and updated software , constructed in 1,150 square feet area.

Library

Well equipped with tons of books which is built in a vast 1,800 square feet area.

Multipurpose Hall

Facilitates many of the school's extracurricular activities. The hall is spread across 4,500 square feet.

Kindergarten play area

A separate play area for the pre-primary divisions.

Canteen

A hygenic and well maintainerd canteen for the ease of students.

Sports

We have two large playgrounds that can accommodate sports such as cricket, football, and hockey. We also have Basketball court, Tennis court and Volleyball court In addition, many more indoor sports can be accommodated.
